Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping usually takes 3-5 business days. Custom PC builds may require an additional 1-2 weeks for assembly and testing before shipment.

Do you offer warranties on custom builds?

Yes! All our custom PCs come with a standard 2-year warranty on parts and labor. Individual components purchased separately carry their manufacturer's warranty.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened items. Custom PCs can be returned within 14 days if there are hardware defects that cannot be resolved.

Can I choose my own parts for a custom build?

Absolutely. Visit our Store page to select compatible components for your dream machine.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to the US, Canada, and select European countries. Shipping costs will be calculated at checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and direct bank transfers for larger custom orders.
